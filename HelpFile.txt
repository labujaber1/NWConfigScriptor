Directions:
1. Click command script in combo box.
2. Double click command in listbox to append
    in editor.
3. Add details in place of ?. 
4. To insert between commands in the editor,
    click command once, click insert button, 
    command will insert at the cursor.
5. Copy script in editor to the clipboard
    by right click in the ditor or highlight text
    and ctl+c. Paste in device CLI.
6. Can save to file "BasicConfig_[date][time]".
